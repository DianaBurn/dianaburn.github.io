1.How long does henna last?

Henna lasts for 7-10 days; it looks fresh for about a week and fades after that,

depending on how fast your skin is renewing itself and on which part of the body

drawing was made. Swimming, washing dishes, and doing

housework can accelerate the fading process.

2.How is henna applied?

Henna is applied to clean, dry skin. Usually it takes form 30 to 60 minutes for me to

draw mehndi, depending on the complexity of the design.

Once it dry, mix of lemon juice and sugar will be applied for the better stain.

Henna paste is left on for a minimum of 3-4 hours,

though overnight is traditional and guarantees the best color.

After 3-4 hours, the paste is gently removed by picking it off or removing the medical tape.

Do not wash it off! The color of mehndi will be orange after removing the paste.

On the next day the color will become darker.

Applying a natural vegetable or beeswax based oil/balm before

bathing can help prolong the life of your henna design.
